◈ Projects

Projects are the heart of CabShop Pro. Every client job gets its own project where you track everything from design to installation.

Creating a project

  1. Click Projects in the sidebar
  2. Click + New Project in the top right
  3. Fill in the project name, client, budget, and dates
  4. Choose a color to make it easy to identify at a glance
  5. Click Save Project
💡 Solo plan users can have up to 10 active projects. Pro and Team plans have unlimited projects.

Logging time on a stage:

  1. Click + Time next to any stage
  2. Either hit ▶ Start to run a live timer, or enter minutes manually
  3. Add a note (optional) and click Log Time

Taking photos on a stage:

  1. Click the 📷 button next to any stage
  2. On mobile: your camera opens directly. On desktop: a file picker opens
  3. The photo uploads to the cloud and appears in the stage's photo gallery
📷 Photo capture is a Pro feature.

Project Financials Tab

The $ Financials tab inside each project shows a complete financial picture:

  • P&L Summary — income vs. expenses at a glance
  • Job Cost vs. Estimate — compares your quoted amount against actual costs with ✅ / ⚠️ indicators
  • ◈ AI Cost Analysis — click to get Claude's analysis of the job's financial performance with recommendations
  • Materials — inventory items assigned to this project
  • Labor — time logged across all stages
  • Transactions — income and expense entries linked to this project
🤖 AI Cost Analysis is a Pro feature.

Quotes Tab (within a Project)

The Quotes tab inside a project shows all quotes and invoices linked to that job. You can also create a new quote directly from here — it will automatically pre-fill the project and client information.

▦ Tasks

Overview

Tasks is a kanban-style board for tracking everything that needs to get done — both shop work and administrative items.

Tasks can be linked to specific projects and contacts, assigned due dates and priorities, broken into subtasks, and added to the calendar.

Task views:

  • Board view — drag and drop cards between To Do / In Progress / Done columns
  • List view — grouped by project, great for a comprehensive overview

Creating a task:

  1. Click + New Task or the + button on any column
  2. Enter a title, description, and due date
  3. Set priority: High Medium Low
  4. Link to a project if applicable
  5. Click Save
💡 Overdue tasks are highlighted in red automatically. Tasks with due dates can be added directly to your Calendar.

📅 Calendar

Overview

The calendar shows all your scheduled events in one view — site visits, deliveries, installations, client meetings, and deadlines.

Event types:

  • Meeting · Site Visit · Deadline · Delivery · Installation · Other

Creating an event:

  1. Click on any date in the calendar
  2. Give the event a title and select the type
  3. Link it to a project and/or contact (optional)
  4. Add a time, duration, and notes
  5. Enable Recurring if it repeats
💡 You can create calendar events directly from a Task (click "Add to Calendar") or from a Contact's profile page.

◑ Quotes & Invoices

Creating a quote

  1. Click Quotes & Invoices → + New Quote
  2. Select the client and link to a project
  3. Add line items — type a name, quantity, unit, and cost per unit
  4. Set markup (% markup, flat amount, or profit margin %)
  5. Add tax rate if applicable
  6. Attach Terms & Conditions from your Resource Library (optional)
  7. Click Save Quote

Unit options:

ea hr day lf sf bf set lot job

Sending for client approval

CabShop Pro lets you send professional approval emails and get digital signatures — no printing, no scanning.

  1. Open the quote and click ✍ Send for Approval
  2. A branded email is sent to the client via SendGrid
  3. The client clicks Review & Approve Quote in the email
  4. They review the full quote, type their name, and draw their signature
  5. They click Approve Quote — the signature is saved
  6. Within 30 seconds, your app updates to show the quote as approved
💡 Approval links expire after 30 days. If a client misses it, use 🔗 Copy Link to resend.
📋 If the client wants changes, they click Decline and enter a reason. You'll see this in the app.

Converting a quote to an invoice

  1. Open an approved quote
  2. Click ⇒ Convert to Invoice
  3. The invoice is created with the same line items — edit as needed
  4. When paid, click ✓ Mark Paid

Quote statuses:

Draft Sent Approved Declined

Invoice statuses:

Unpaid Partial Paid Overdue

Item Library

The Item Library stores reusable line items so you don't have to re-enter common items every time you write a quote.

  1. Click Quotes & Invoices → ⊞ Item Library
  2. Click + New Item and fill in the details
  3. When building a quote, click From Library to insert saved items instantly
💡 You can link library items to inventory for automatic cost updates when material prices change.

$ Finance Tracker

Overview

The Finance Tracker gives you a real-time view of your shop's financial health — income, expenses, and profitability by project.

Views:

  • Ledger — all transactions in chronological order
  • By Account — grouped by chart of accounts category
  • Pipeline — shows Collected, Outstanding, Booked, and Pipeline revenue at a glance

Adding a transaction:

  1. Click + Add Transaction
  2. Choose Income or Expense
  3. Enter amount, date, description, and account code
  4. Link to a project (optional but recommended)
  5. Click Save

Downloading reports:

Click the Download CSV button to export: Full Ledger, P&L, Income Only, or Expenses Only. These can be imported into accounting software or shared with your accountant.

⊞ Inventory

Overview

Track every material and piece of hardware in your shop. Get automatic alerts when stock falls below your minimum reorder quantity.

Adding an inventory item:

  1. Click Inventory → + New Item
  2. Enter the item name, category, unit cost, and quantity on hand
  3. Set a minimum reorder quantity — you'll get an alert when stock drops below this
  4. Link to a supplier contact (optional — enables direct ordering)
  5. Click Save

Logging usage:

When you use materials on a job, click Log Usage on the item and link it to a project. This deducts from your stock count and records the cost against the project.

Restocking:

Click Restock to add inventory and log the purchase cost.

💡 Low stock items appear as alerts on your Dashboard so you never run out mid-job.

🔧 Tools & Equipment

Overview

Keep a complete record of every tool and piece of equipment in your shop — purchase dates, serial numbers, warranties, and service history.

  • Log service and maintenance events with dates and notes
  • Attach documents (manuals, receipts) to each tool
  • Track warranty expiration dates
  • Categorize by type (Power Tools, Hand Tools, CNC, Finishing, etc.)

◫ Library

Documents (Resource Library)

Store client specifications, SOPs, templates, terms & conditions, and reference documents. Attach documents to specific projects or keep them as shop-wide resources.

📄 Library features are available on Pro and Team plans.

Media Library

A central place for all project photos, videos, and documents. Tag items with projects and custom labels. Filter by file type or project.

Gallery

A visual portfolio of completed project photos, organized by project. Great for showcasing your work to clients or building a marketing library.

Samples Library

Store your finish and material samples — door styles, wood species, paint colors, hardware, edge profiles. Each sample has a description, tags, swatch color, and photo. Share with clients during the design phase.

◈ AI Features

CabShop Assistant (AI Chatbot)

Look for the 💬 button in the bottom right corner of the app. Click it to open your AI assistant, which knows every feature in CabShop Pro.

Ask it anything:

  • "How do I log time on a project stage?"
  • "How do I convert a quote to an invoice?"
  • "How does inventory reordering work?"
  • "How do I send a quote for approval?"
🤖 AI Assistant is available on Pro and Team plans.

AI Cost Analysis

Inside any project, go to the $ Financials tab and scroll to the Job Cost vs. Estimate section. Click ◈ AI Cost Analysis to get Claude's assessment of the job's financial performance, including:

  • Assessment of whether the job is on track
  • Likely causes of any cost variances
  • Specific recommendations to improve profitability on similar jobs

You can save the analysis directly to the project's Notes tab.

🤖 AI Cost Analysis is available on Pro and Team plans.
